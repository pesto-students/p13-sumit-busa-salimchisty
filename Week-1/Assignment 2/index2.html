Bokaro, Jharkhand, India

Location

Bokaro the city of steel is located in a state of jharkhand, India

About

Bokaro, officially known as Bokaro Steel City, is a large and planned city in Jharkhand, India. It is the fourth most populous city in the state and one of the planned cities of India. It is one of the most peaceful cities in India.

Weather

In Bokaro, the climate is warm and temperate. The summers are much rainier than the winters in Bokaro.

Population

As per 2011 census, the city's population was 563,417 and 1,100,000 of its metropolitan area. The city span across 183 km in geographic area.
